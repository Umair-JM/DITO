DITO runs a real evaluation fully offline on your device — no account, no API key, no data leaves your phone. You can optionally let a free public AI add a richer second opinion when you're online.

When ON, your summary is sent to Pollinations.ai — a free, keyless, open public AI service that anyone can use. No sign-up and no API keys are involved (none of the app author's keys either). If it's slow or unavailable, DITO silently falls back to the offline engine.

Privacy: with the boost OFF, nothing ever leaves your device. With it ON, only the text you type is sent to that third-party service for scoring.
